Sendero Digital is an e-learning platform built for members of the Church of the Nazarene in México. It gives congregations a single place to take courses, follow lessons at their own pace, and pick up where they left off, all in Spanish and on whatever device they happen to have on hand.

I built the whole thing from scratch: the design, the frontend, and the backend that keeps courses, lessons, and student progress in order. It was mine to figure out end to end, from the data model up to the last detail of the interface, and it's the kind of project where every decision had to actually hold up for real people using it every week.
